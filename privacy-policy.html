Privacy Policy for EMI Loan Calculator

Last Updated: May 22, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to EMI Loan Calculator. We are committed to respecting your privacy. This simple Privacy Policy explains our approach to protecting your information.

2. No Information Collection

We do not collect any personal information from users of our Loan Calculator. The calculator is designed to function entirely within your browser, and any information you enter (such as loan amounts, interest rates, etc.) is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to or stored on our servers.

3. How the Calculator Works

The Loan Calculator is a simple tool that helps you determine loan repayments based on the information you input. All calculations are performed within your browser, and the results are displayed immediately. No data is sent to our servers during this process.

4. Cookies

Our website may use essential cookies that are necessary for the website to function properly. These cookies do not collect personal information and are used only to enhance your user experience.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top.
