Privacy Policy for Our Apps

Last Updated: June 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Privacy Policy for all mobile applications developed and published by Rayzen Studios. We are committed to respecting your privacy and protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how our apps handle information and what third-party services may be used across our portfolio.

2. How Our Apps Work

Many of our applications perform processing locally on your device and do not require you to create an account. Depending on the specific app and its features, certain information may be shared with third-party service providers that support functionality such as advertising, analytics, crash reporting, or app performance monitoring. Any such data collection is limited to what is necessary for those services to operate.

3. Advertising and Third-Party Services

Some of our applications may display advertisements to support development and maintenance. Apps that do not contain advertising do not use advertising-related services.

For apps that display ads, we may use third-party advertising providers such as Google AdMob and other advertising partners. These services may collect limited device and usage information in accordance with their own privacy policies in order to provide, measure, and improve advertising.

Depending on your device settings, operating system, and region, advertising providers may use identifiers such as the Advertising Identifier (IDFA on iOS) or other advertising-related information to deliver personalized or non-personalized advertisements. Where required by law, we will request your consent before enabling personalized advertising.

We do not sell your personal information. Any information collected by third-party advertising providers is governed by their respective privacy policies and practices.

Google AdMob Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Cookies

Our website may use essential cookies that are necessary for the website to function properly. These cookies do not collect personal information and are used only to enhance your user experience.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the contact information provided on our website or app store listings.
